How To Reach Us

IIT Jodhpur Hostels, Kendranchal Colony, Vivek Vihar, Pali Road, JODHPUR, RAJASTHAN.

From Airport

You can take a prepaid auto/taxi to the GPRA Residential Campus, which is situated in Vivek Vihar (Near Indian Oil Residential Complex and Bharat Petroleum Residential Area) on Pali Road to the south of the Jodhpur city. It is about 20 km from the airport and takes about 20 minutes to reach.

From Railway Station

At Jodhpur Junction Railway Station, exit from Platform No.1 side. You can take a prepaid auto/taxi to the GPRA Residential Campus, which is situated in Vivek Vihar (Near Indian Oil Residential Complex and Bharat Petroleum Residential Area) on Pali Road to the south of the Jodhpur city. You may ask the driver to take you to Jhalamand Chauraha on Pali Road, from where this campus is 3 km on NH 65 towards Pali.
